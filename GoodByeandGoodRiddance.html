Juice WRLDs Story

This was his debut album which released May 23rd, 2018 by Grade A Productions and Interscope Records.

This album includes some of his biggest and most recognizable songs such as Lucid Dreams and All Girls Are The Same.

The album has a total of 17 tracks with 3 being skits and the other 14 being his songs.

This was his first album to hit platinum and all of the songs on it have officially hit Platinum or better.
