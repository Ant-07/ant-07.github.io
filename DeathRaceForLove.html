Juice WRLDs Story

This album was Juice WRLD's second debut album being released March 8th, 2019.

It featured 22 songs with no interludes but 1 with Demonz being a snippet of a song he was working on with Brent Faiyaz.

It is a Rock and Hip Hop genre album but also features songs that hint towards wanting to be left alone, understanding and accepting others Scars, Trauma, Past experiences, and Flaws and Sins.

It debuted at number one on the US Billboard 200 chart becoming the artists first ever number-one album.

This album also features Young Thug, Rvssian, Clever, Youngboy Never Broke Again, and Brent Faiyaz.
