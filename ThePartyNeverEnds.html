Juice WRLDs Story

This is Juice WRLDs last album released on November 30th, 2024 released by his manager lilbibby, Grade A Productions, and Interscope Records.

This album has 19 songs most of which were unreleased until this albums release as well as having no interludes.

All the songs on this album had sound clips from past concerts or sounds the artist himself recorded before his tragic death that are included at the end of or the beginning of all the songs.

This album also has features from Fall Out Boy, Offset, Eminem, benny blanco, Eminem, Nicki Minaj, and The Kid Laroi had his own song on the album as a tribute to Juice WRLD, Tupac, XXXTentacion, Micheal Jackson, and other legends in Music,

This album also was paired up with a live event in Fortnite that had a tribute to Juice WRLD with his last message at the end being "I want to change the world -Juice WRLD"
